Overview

The goal of this assignment was to work in a group of three to design and build a pinhole camera. With our build, we painted over the entirety of the inside of a shoebox with black acrylic paint in order to prevent any light from reflecting in the box. We duct taped the sides of the box to keep any unwanted light from entering our camera. To measure the differences between aperture sizes, we poked 3 different sized holes (1mm, 3mm, 5mm) into cardstock. In lieu of a digital camera, we used a cellphone camera and set the shutter speed to 23.5 seconds and decreased ISO to 100 in order to decrease image noise.
